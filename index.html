Frequently Asked Questions

Do you require reservations?

While we do accommodate walk-ins when possible, we highly recommend making a reservation in advance to guarantee your table, especially on weekends.

What is the dress code?

We kindly request our guests to dress in smart casual or elegant evening wear. Athletic wear and flip-flops are not permitted in the main dining room.

Do you cater to dietary restrictions?

Absolutely. Our culinary team is happy to accommodate vegan, gluten-free, and allergy-specific requests. Please mention these when booking.

Can I host a private event?

Yes, Foodies offers an exclusive private dining room perfect for corporate gatherings or intimate celebrations. Contact our events team for details.
